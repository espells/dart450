Once upon a time there was a student who loved playing with blocks.
She loved all kinds of blocks but her personal favorite kind of block were Legos.
Legos were really fun, but occasionally they were very frustrating. Nothing hurt more than running into the family room and having your heel meet with the sharp plastic cube that is a lego.
But she prevailed, building Lego creations for her Lego people.
But as time went on, her love for legos wasn't as strong and her beloved Legos didn't see her ever again.
It is rumored that she continues building but not with Legos. Tragic.
